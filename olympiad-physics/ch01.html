一、牛顿力学

1. 运动、动力学

① 线量：

$$ \vec{s} = \vec{r}_t - \vec{r}_0, \quad \vec{v} = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt}, \quad \vec{a} = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} = \frac{d^2\vec{r}}{dt^2} $$

（自然系中 $\vec{a} = \frac{dv}{dt}\hat{\tau} + \frac{v^2}{\rho}\hat{n}$）

② 角量：

$$ \theta = \varphi_2 - \varphi_1, \quad \omega = \frac{d\varphi}{dt}, \quad \beta = \frac{d\omega}{dt} = \frac{d^2\varphi}{dt^2}, \quad \vec{v} = \vec{\omega} \times \vec{r} $$

曲率半径求法：

1) $\rho = \frac{ds}{d\theta} = \frac{v^3}{|\vec{v}\times\vec{a}|} = \frac{v^2}{a_n}$

2) $v = \frac{\sqrt{(dx)^2+(dy)^2}}{dt}$

$$ a_n = a_x \sin\theta - a_y \cos\theta = \frac{d^2x}{dt^2}\frac{dy}{dx}\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+(\frac{dy}{dx})^2}} - \frac{d^2y}{dt^2}\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+(\frac{dy}{dx})^2}} $$

因为 $v = \frac{ds}{dt}$，

$$ \therefore \rho = \frac{v^2}{a_n} = \frac{[(dx)^2+(dy)^2]^{3/2}}{|dx\,d^2y - dy\,d^2x|} $$

对于椭圆 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1$，有：

$$ \rho = \frac{[(dx)^2+\frac{b^2}{a^2}\frac{x^2dx^2}{a^2-x^2}]^{3/2}}{\frac{b}{a}(\sqrt{a^2-x^2} + \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}})dx(dx^2)} = \frac{(1+\frac{b^2}{a^2}\frac{x^2}{a^2-x^2})^{3/2}}{\frac{b}{a}\frac{a^2}{a^2-x^2}} = \frac{(a^4-c^2x^2)^{3/2}}{a^4b} $$

（当 $x=0$ 时, $\rho = \frac{a^2}{b}$； 当 $x=a$ 时, $\rho = \frac{b^2}{a}$）

③ 典型例：

1) 匀速直线：$\vec{s} = \vec{v} t$

2) 匀加速直线：$\vec{v} = \vec{a}t + \vec{v}_0$, $\vec{s} = \int \vec{v} dt = \vec{s}_0 + \vec{v}_0 t + \frac{1}{2}\vec{a}t^2$

3) 圆周：

$$ \vec{a}_n = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} $$

若 $\vec{a}_t$ 为一常量，则 $\omega = \beta t + \omega_0$, $\theta = \theta_0 + \omega_0 t + \frac{1}{2}\beta t^2$

4) $F = \frac{GmM}{r^2}$ 的椭圆：

设 $v_\theta$ 为横向速度， $v_r$ 为径向速度。

$$ \begin{cases} L = m v_\theta r \\ E = \frac{1}{2}m v^2 - \frac{GmM}{r} = \frac{1}{2}m(v_\theta^2 + v_r^2) - \frac{GmM}{r} \end{cases} $$

在A、B两点， $v_r = 0$, $v = \frac{L}{mr_0}$：

$$ \therefore E = \frac{1}{2}m \frac{L^2}{m^2r_0^2} - \frac{GmM}{r_0} $$

整理得：

$$ r_0^2 + \frac{GmM}{E}r_0 - \frac{L^2}{2mE} = 0 $$

又 $\because$ 在A、B两点 $r_0 = a+c$ 或 $a-c$：

$$ \begin{cases} r_1 + r_2 = 2a = -\frac{GmM}{E} \\ r_1 r_2 = a^2 - c^2 = b^2 = -\frac{L^2}{2mE} \end{cases} $$

解得：

$$ E = -\frac{GmM}{2a} \Rightarrow a = -\frac{GmM}{2E} $$ $$ L = mb\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}} \Rightarrow b = \sqrt{-\frac{L^2}{2mE}} $$

故椭圆方程为：

$$ \frac{x^2}{(-\frac{GmM}{2E})^2} + \frac{y^2}{(-\frac{L^2}{2mE})} = 1 $$

曲率半径：

$$ \frac{GmM}{r^2} \cos\theta = m\frac{v^2}{\rho} $$

而 $m v \cos\theta \cdot r = m b \sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}} \Rightarrow \cos\theta = \frac{b}{vr}\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}}$

$$ \therefore \rho = \frac{r^2v^2}{GM\cos\theta} = \frac{r^3v^3}{GMb\sqrt{GM/a}} $$

而 $\frac{1}{2}mv^2 - \frac{GmM}{r} = -\frac{GmM}{2a} \Rightarrow v = \sqrt{GM(\frac{2}{r}-\frac{1}{a})}$

$$ \therefore \rho = \frac{r^3 (GM)^{3/2} (\frac{2}{r}-\frac{1}{a})^{3/2}}{(GM)^{3/2} b \frac{1}{\sqrt{a}}} = \frac{r^3}{b}\sqrt{a}\left(\frac{2}{r}-\frac{1}{a}\right)^{3/2} $$

A点：$\rho_A = \frac{(a-c)^3}{b} \sqrt{a} \left(\frac{2}{a-c}-\frac{1}{a}\right)^{3/2} = \frac{b^2}{a}$

B点：$\rho_B = \frac{(a+c)^3}{b} \sqrt{a} \left(\frac{2}{a+c}-\frac{1}{a}\right)^{3/2} = \frac{b^2}{a}$

C点：$\rho_C = \frac{a^3}{b} \sqrt{a} \left(\frac{2}{a}-\frac{1}{a}\right)^{3/2} = \frac{a^2}{b}$

周期：由于单位时间矢径扫过的面积 $\frac{dS}{dt} = \frac{1}{2}|\vec{r}\times\vec{v}| = \frac{1}{2}r v_\theta = \frac{1}{2}rv\cos\theta$

$\because v\cos\theta = \frac{b}{r}\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}}$

$$ \therefore \frac{dS}{dt} = \frac{1}{2}r \cdot \frac{b}{r}\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}} = \frac{b}{2}\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}} $$ $$ \therefore \pi ab = \frac{b}{2}\sqrt{\frac{GM}{a}} \cdot T \Rightarrow T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{a^3}{GM}} $$

5) 抛体运动：

$$ v_x = v_0\cos\theta, \quad v_y = v_0\sin\theta - gt $$ $$ x = v_0\cos\theta \cdot t, \quad y = v_0\sin\theta \cdot t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2 $$

射程 $X = \frac{v_0^2\sin2\theta}{g}$，射高 $Y = \frac{v_0^2\sin^2\theta}{2g}$

轨道方程：消去$t$得

$$ y = x\tan\theta - \frac{gx^2}{2v_0^2\cos^2\theta} = -\frac{g}{2v_0^2\cos^2\theta}x^2 + \tan\theta \cdot x $$

（此方程中参数有 $x, y, v_0, \tan\theta$ ，知二可求四，知二可求最）

曲率半径：$\rho = \frac{v^2}{a_n} = \frac{v_0^2 - 2gy}{g\cos\alpha} = \frac{(v_0^2 - 2v_0gy\sin\theta + g^2t^2)^{3/2}}{g v_0\cos\theta}$

包络线方程1：初速度各向同：

$$ \begin{cases} x = \frac{v_0^2\sin2\theta}{2g} \quad \text{(最远)} \\ y = \frac{v_0^2\sin^2\theta}{2g} = \frac{v_0^2}{4g}(1-\cos2\theta) \quad \text{(最高)} \end{cases} $$

消去$\theta$得：

$$ \left(\frac{2g}{v_0^2}x\right)^2 + \left(1 - \frac{4g}{v_0^2}y\right)^2 = 1 $$

变形得：

$$ \frac{x^2}{(\frac{v_0^2}{2g})^2} + \frac{(y - \frac{v_0^2}{4g})^2}{(\frac{v_0^2}{4g})^2} = 1 $$

中心坐标：$(0, \frac{v_0^2}{4g})$， $a = \frac{v_0^2}{2g}$, $b = \frac{v_0^2}{4g}$

包络线方程2：同方向，各向速度：

$$ \begin{cases} x = \frac{v^2\sin2\theta}{2g} \quad \text{(最远)} \\ y = \frac{v^2}{4g}(1-\cos2\theta) \quad \text{(最高)} \end{cases} $$

消去$v^2$：

$$ \frac{y}{x} = \frac{1}{2}\frac{1-\cos2\theta}{\sin2\theta} = \frac{1}{2}\tan\theta $$

6) 简谐运动：

<1> 弹簧振子：

$$ \vec{F} = -k\vec{x} = m\frac{d^2\vec{x}}{dt^2} $$ $$ \therefore kx + m\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} = 0 \Rightarrow kx\,dx + m\frac{dx}{dt}d\left(\frac{dx}{dt}\right) = 0 $$

积分得：

$$ \frac{1}{2}kx^2 - \frac{1}{2}kA^2 + \frac{1}{2}m\left(\frac{dx}{dt}\right)^2 = 0 $$ $$ \therefore \frac{dx}{\sqrt{A^2-x^2}} = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} dt $$

令 $x = A\cos\theta$，代入上式得 $\theta = -\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}t$

$$ \therefore x = A\cos\left(\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}t\right) \Rightarrow T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}} $$ $$ \begin{cases} v = \frac{dx}{dt} = -A\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}\sin\left(\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}t\right) \\ a = \frac{dv}{dt} = -A\frac{k}{m}\cos\left(\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}t\right) \end{cases} $$

<2> 单摆：

$$ \vec{F} = -mg\sin\varphi \cdot \hat{x} - mg\cos\varphi \cdot \hat{\tau} = m\frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} $$ $$ mgl(1-\cos\varphi) = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 + mgl(1-\cos\theta) $$ $$ \therefore v = \sqrt{2gl(\cos\theta-\cos\varphi)} \approx \sqrt{gl(\varphi^2-\theta^2)} $$

令 $\theta = \varphi\cos\alpha$，代入上式得 $v = \sqrt{gl}\varphi\sin\alpha$

$$ \because dv = -\sqrt{gl}\frac{\theta\,d\theta}{\sqrt{\varphi^2-\theta^2}} $$

或者由 $\frac{d\theta}{dt} = -\frac{v}{l}$：

$$ dt = \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}\frac{d\theta}{\sqrt{\varphi^2-\theta^2}} = \sqrt{\frac{l}{g}}\frac{-\varphi\sin\alpha\,d\alpha}{\varphi\sin\alpha} = -\sqrt{\frac{l}{g}} d\alpha $$ $$ \therefore \alpha = \sqrt{\frac{g}{l}}t \quad \text{(代入前式得)} $$ $$ \begin{cases} \theta = \varphi\cos\left(\sqrt{\frac{g}{l}}t\right) \Rightarrow T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{l}{g}} \\ v = -\varphi\sqrt{gl}\sin\left(\sqrt{\frac{g}{l}}t\right) \\ a = -\varphi g\cos\left(\sqrt{\frac{g}{l}}t\right) \end{cases} $$

<3> 复摆：

$$ M \times (-mgh\theta) = I\frac{d\omega}{dt} $$ $$ mgh(1-\cos\varphi) = \frac{1}{2}I\omega^2 + mgh(1-\cos\theta) $$

同理 $T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{I}{mgh}}$

7) 圆轮运动：(纯滚)

在梯形 $AB P' P$ 中：$AB = x = P P' - R\sin\varphi = R(\varphi - \sin\varphi)$

在梯形 $B C O' P'$ 中：$BC = y = P' O' - R\cos\varphi = R(1 - \cos\varphi)$

曲率半径：$\vec{a} = \frac{d|v|}{dt}\hat{\tau} + \frac{v^2}{\rho}\hat{n}$

$$ \frac{d|v|}{dt} = \frac{v^2}{R}, \quad \text{而} \quad v_{A}^2 = 2v^2(1-\cos\varphi) $$ $$ \therefore \rho = \frac{(2v^2(1-\cos\varphi))^{3/2}}{v^2/R \cdot \sin(\varphi/2)} = 4R\sin\frac{\varphi}{2} $$

8) 重力场与磁场结合问题

质量为$m$，带电量为$+q$的粒子从原点无初速度释放。

给粒子附加沿x轴正方向和负方向各一个速度$v$，使 $qvB = mg$。

$$ \therefore v = \frac{mg}{qB}, \quad R = \frac{mv}{qB} = \frac{m^2g}{q^2B^2} $$

粒子做水平向右匀速直线运动和匀速圆周运动。

$$ \therefore x = vt - R\sin\omega t, \quad y = R - R\cos\omega t $$ $$ \because v = \frac{mg}{qB} = \frac{qBR}{m} \therefore \omega = \frac{v}{R} = \frac{qB}{m} $$ $$ \therefore \begin{cases} x = \frac{mg}{qB}t - R\sin(\frac{qB}{m}t) = \frac{mg}{qB}t - \frac{m^2g}{q^2B^2}\sin(\frac{qB}{m}t) \\ y = R - R\cos(\frac{qB}{m}t) = \frac{m^2g}{q^2B^2} - \frac{m^2g}{q^2B^2}\cos(\frac{qB}{m}t) \end{cases} $$

9) 波动

$$ \begin{cases} \text{横波: 振动方向与传播方向垂直 (固体中传播)} \\ \text{纵波: 振动方向与传播方向相同 (固、液、气中传播)} \end{cases} $$ $$ y(x,t) = A\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x + \varphi\right) $$ $$ = A\cos(\omega t - kx + \varphi) \quad k \text{: 波数} $$ $$ = A\cos 2\pi\left(\frac{t}{T} - \frac{x}{\lambda} + \frac{\varphi}{2\pi}\right) $$

当 $x=x_0$ 时，方程表示该点振动方程：

$$ y(t) = A\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x_0 + \varphi\right) $$

当 $t=t_0$ 时，方程表示该时刻波形：

$$ y(x) = A\cos\left(\omega t_0 - \frac{\omega}{v}x + \varphi\right) $$

<1> 波速：

$$ \frac{\partial^2 y(x,t)}{\partial t^2} = -\omega^2 A\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x + \varphi\right) $$ $$ \frac{\partial^2 y(x,t)}{\partial x^2} = -\frac{\omega^2}{v^2} A\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x + \varphi\right) $$ $$ \therefore \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial t^2} = v^2 \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial x^2} $$

轻质柔软弦的横波方程：

$$ \begin{cases} T_2 \sin\alpha_2 - T_1 \sin\alpha_1 = \rho \, ds \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial t^2} \\ T_2 \cos\alpha_2 - T_1 \cos\alpha_1 = 0 \end{cases} $$ $$ \because \begin{cases} \cos\alpha_1 \approx \cos\alpha_2 \approx 1 \\ \sin\alpha_1 \approx \tan\alpha_1 = \left.\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\right|_x, \quad \sin\alpha_2 \approx \tan\alpha_2 = \left.\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\right|_{x+dx} \end{cases} $$ $$ \therefore \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial t^2} = \frac{T}{\rho} \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial x^2} $$ $$ \therefore v = \sqrt{\frac{T}{\rho}} $$

稀薄气体中的声速：

气体可以为在传播过程中经历绝热过程， $PV^\gamma = C$

$$ \therefore V^\gamma dp + \gamma PV^{\gamma-1} dV = 0 \Rightarrow \frac{dp}{dV} = -\gamma \frac{P}{V} $$

由体变模量定义： $dp = -B \frac{dV}{V} \therefore B = -V \frac{dp}{dV} = \gamma P$

$$ \therefore v = \sqrt{\frac{B}{\rho}} = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma P}{\rho}} = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma RT}{M}} $$

<2> 波的能量和能流：

$$ \begin{cases} \text{动能：} dE_k = \frac{1}{2} dm \cdot v^2 = \frac{1}{2} \rho \, dV \left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial t}\right)^2 = \frac{1}{2} \rho \, dV \, \omega^2 A^2 \sin^2\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x\right) \\ \text{势能：} \frac{F}{S} = Y \frac{dy}{dx} \Rightarrow F = \frac{YS}{dx} dy = k \, dy \end{cases} $$ $$ dE_p = \frac{1}{2}k(dy)^2 = \frac{1}{2}\frac{YS}{dx}(dy)^2 = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\right)^2(S \, dx) \cdot Y = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\right)^2 \cdot Y \, dV $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2} \rho \, dV \, \omega^2 A^2 \sin^2\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x\right) $$ $$ \therefore dE = dE_k + dE_p = \rho \, dV \, \omega^2 A^2 \sin^2\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x\right) $$

对于平面简谐波：

能量密度 $\varepsilon = \frac{dE}{dV} = \rho \, \omega^2 A^2 \sin^2(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x)$

能流密度 $\vec{I} = \varepsilon \vec{v}$ ， $\bar{I} = \bar{\varepsilon}v = \frac{1}{2} \rho A^2 \omega^2 v$

对于球面波：

$$ \bar{I}_1 4\pi r_1^2 = \bar{I}_2 4\pi r_2^2 \therefore \frac{\bar{I}_1}{\bar{I}_2} = \frac{r_2^2}{r_1^2} = \frac{A_1^2}{A_2^2} \therefore y = \frac{A_0 r_0}{r}\cos(\omega t - kr) $$

<3> 波的干涉：

$$ y_1(x,t) = A_1\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x_1 + \varphi_1\right) $$ $$ y_2(x,t) = A_2\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{\omega}{v}x_2 + \varphi_2\right) $$

在某点P，两列波的合成仍为简谐运动，有

$$ y(t) = y_1(x_1, t) + y_2(x_2, t) = A\cos(\omega t + \varphi) $$ $$ \Delta \varphi = (\varphi_1 - \varphi_2) - \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}(x_1 - x_2) $$ $$ A = \sqrt{A_1^2 + A_2^2 + 2A_1 A_2 \cos\Delta\varphi} $$

当 $\Delta\varphi = 2k\pi$ 时，干涉相长， $A = A_1 + A_2$

当 $\Delta\varphi = (2k+1)\pi$ 时，干涉相消， $A = |A_1 - A_2|$

<4> 驻波：实质为波的干涉

左行波 $y_1(x,t) = A\cos\left(\omega t + \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}x\right)$

右行波 $y_2(x,t) = A\cos\left(\omega t - \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}x\right)$

$$ \therefore y(x,t) = y_1(x,t) + y_2(x,t) = 2A\cos\left(\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}x\right) \cos\omega t $$ $$ \begin{cases} \text{当 } \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}x = k\pi \text{，即 } x = k\frac{\lambda}{2} \text{ 时，振幅取得最大，为波腹} \\ \text{当 } \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}x = (2k+1)\frac{\pi}{2} \text{，即 } x = (2k+1)\frac{\lambda}{4} \text{ 时，振幅为零，为波节} \end{cases} $$ $$ \begin{cases} \text{绳波中两个端点必处是驻波节点，故绳长 } L = k\cdot\frac{\lambda}{2} \\ \text{核外电子绕原子核的运动为物质波，轨道周长满足 } 2\pi R = k\lambda \end{cases} $$

④ 质点动力学：

瞬时效应：$\vec{F} = m\vec{a} = m\frac{d\vec{v}}{dt}$

时间积累：$\vec{F} dt = m d\vec{v} \Rightarrow \int_{t_1}^{t_2} \vec{F} dt = m(\vec{v}_2 - \vec{v}_1)$

空间积累：$\vec{F} \cdot d\vec{s} = m\vec{v} \cdot d\vec{v} \Rightarrow \int_{s_1}^{s_2} \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{s} = \frac{1}{2}m(v_2^2 - v_1^2)$

$$ P = \frac{dW}{dt} = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{v} $$

⑤ 刚体动力学：

$$ \vec{M} = \vec{r} \times \vec{F} $$

瞬时效应：$M = I\beta = I\frac{d\omega}{dt}$ （$I = \int r^2 dm$，$r$ 为 $dm$ 到转轴的距离）

时间积累：$M dt = I d\omega \Rightarrow \int_{t_1}^{t_2} M dt = I(\omega_2 - \omega_1)$

空间积累：$M d\theta = I\omega d\omega \Rightarrow \int_{\theta_1}^{\theta_2} M d\theta = \frac{1}{2}I(\omega_2^2 - \omega_1^2)$

$$ P = \frac{dW}{dt} = \vec{M} \cdot \vec{\omega} $$

1) 转动惯量平行轴定理： $I = I_c + md^2$

（$I_c$：刚体对过质心转轴的转动惯量，$I$：刚体对于过质心转轴相距 $l$ 的另一条平行转轴的转动惯量）

$$ I = \int r^2 dm = \int (\vec{r}_c - \vec{l})^2 dm = I_c + ml^2 - 2\vec{l}\cdot\int \vec{r}_c dm = I_c + ml^2 $$

2) 转动惯量垂直轴定理： $I_z = I_x + I_y$

$$ I_z = \int r^2 dm = \int (x^2 + y^2) dm = \int x^2 dm + \int y^2 dm = I_x + I_y $$

3) 典型几何体转动惯量：

<1> 圆环

$$ I_1 = \int r^2 dm = mR^2 $$

<2> 圆盘

$$ I_2 = \int_0^R r^2 \frac{2\pi r dr}{\pi R^2} m = \frac{2m}{R^2}\int_0^R r^3 dr = \frac{1}{2}mR^2 $$

<3> 圆筒

$$ I_3 = \int_{r_1}^{r_2} r^2 \frac{2\pi r dr}{\pi(r_2^2-r_1^2)} m = \frac{2m}{r_2^2-r_1^2} \int_{r_1}^{r_2} r^3 dr = \frac{1}{2}m(r_1^2+r_2^2) $$

<4> 细杆

① 绕中心轴：

$$ I_1 = \int_{-L/2}^{L/2} r^2 \frac{m}{L} dr = \frac{1}{12}mL^2 $$

② 绕端点轴：

$$ I_2 = I_1 + m\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^2 = \frac{1}{3}mL^2 $$

<5> 圆柱体

$$ I_1 = \int_0^R r^2 \frac{2\pi r dr \cdot l}{\pi R^2 l} m = \frac{1}{2}mR^2 $$ $$ I_2 = \int \left[ \frac{dm \cdot l^2}{12} + dm(R\sin\theta)^2 \right] = \frac{1}{12}ml^2 + 2\int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{(R\cos\theta)(R d\theta\cos\theta)}{\pi R^2} m (R\sin\theta)^2 $$ $$ = \frac{1}{12}ml^2 + \frac{4mR^2}{\pi} \int_0^{\pi/2} \cos^2\theta \sin^2\theta d\theta $$ $$ = \frac{1}{12}ml^2 + \frac{1}{4}mR^2 $$

<6> 球体与球壳

① 实心球：

$$ I_1 = \int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{(\pi R^2\cos^2\theta)(R d\theta\cos\theta)}{\frac{4}{3}\pi R^3} m (R\sin\theta)^2 = 3mR^2 \int_0^{\pi/2} \cos^3\theta \sin^2\theta d\theta = \frac{2}{5}mR^2 $$

② 球壳：

$$ I_2 = 2\int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{2\pi R\cos\theta \cdot R d\theta}{4\pi R^2} m (R\sin\theta)^2 = mR^2 \int_0^{\pi/2} \sin^2\theta \cos\theta d\theta = \frac{2}{3}mR^2 $$

⑥ 碰撞：

1) 正碰：

$$ \begin{cases} m_1v_1 + m_2v_2 = m_1v_1' + m_2v_2' \\ v_2' - v_1' = e(v_1 - v_2) \end{cases} $$ $$ \begin{cases} e = 1 \text{ 完全弹性体} \\ e \in (0, 1) \text{ 一般非弹性体} \\ e = 0 \text{ 完全非弹性体} \end{cases} $$

解得：

$$ v_1' = \frac{m_1v_1+m_2v_2}{m_1+m_2} - \frac{m_2}{m_1+m_2}e(v_1-v_2) $$ $$ v_2' = \frac{m_1v_1+m_2v_2}{m_1+m_2} + \frac{m_1}{m_1+m_2}e(v_1-v_2) $$

第一项为质心速度：$\frac{m_1v_1+m_2v_2}{m_1+m_2}$，由于动量守恒，这一项不变。

第二项为相对质心的运动速度，能量在质心系中两二体作用而损耗。

损失的能量：

$$ \Delta E = \frac{1}{2}m_1v_1^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2v_2^2 - \frac{1}{2}m_1v_1'^2 - \frac{1}{2}m_2v_2'^2 $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2}\frac{m_1m_2}{m_1+m_2}(v_1-v_2)^2 - \frac{1}{2}\frac{m_1m_2}{m_1+m_2}(v_1'-v_2')^2 $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2}(1-e^2)\frac{m_1m_2}{m_1+m_2}(v_1-v_2)^2 $$

2) 斜碰：

$$ v_{2x}' - v_{1x}' = e(v_1\cos\theta_1 - v_2\cos\theta_2) $$ $$ m_1v_1\cos\theta_1 + m_2v_2\cos\theta_2 = m_1v_{1x}' + m_2v_{2x}' $$ $$ -m_1v_1\cos\theta_1 + m_1v_{1x}' = N \Delta t_1 $$ $$ -m_1v_1\sin\theta_1 + m_1v_{1y}' = \mu N \Delta t_1 $$ $$ m_1v_1\sin\theta_1 + m_2v_2\sin\theta_2 = m_1v_{1y}' + m_2v_{2y}' $$

⑦ 万有引力：

1) 万有引力作用下质点的运动轨迹：

由上图， $\frac{y}{x} = \tan\alpha$， $\frac{dy}{dx} = \tan[\pi-(\theta-\alpha)] = \tan(\alpha-\theta) = \frac{\tan\alpha-\tan\theta}{1+\tan\alpha\tan\theta}$ <1>

由于万有引力是保守力：$E = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 - \frac{GmM}{r}$ 为常量。

由于万有引力是有心力：$L = mvr\sin\theta$ 为常量。

$$ \therefore E = \frac{1}{2}m\frac{L^2}{m^2r^2\sin^2\theta} - \frac{GmM}{r} = \frac{L^2(1+\tan^2\theta)}{2mr^2\tan^2\theta} - \frac{GmM}{r} = \frac{L^2}{2mr^2\tan^2\theta} + \frac{L^2}{2mr^2} - \frac{GmM}{r} $$

解得：

$$ \tan^2\theta = \frac{L^2}{2mr^2} / \left(E + \frac{GmM}{r} - \frac{L^2}{2mr^2}\right) = \frac{L^2}{2mr^2E + 2GmMm^2r - L^2} $$ $$ \therefore \tan\theta = \frac{L}{\sqrt{2mE\left[\left(r+\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 + \frac{L^2}{2mE}\right]}} \quad \text{<2>} $$

由 $y = r\sin\alpha$, $x = r\cos\alpha$， <1>式变为：

$$ \frac{\sin\alpha \, dr + r\cos\alpha \, d\alpha}{\cos\alpha \, dr - r\sin\alpha \, d\alpha} = \frac{\tan\alpha - \tan\theta}{1 + \tan\alpha\tan\theta} = -\frac{1}{\tan\theta} + \frac{\frac{1}{\tan\alpha}+\tan\alpha}{1+\tan\alpha\tan\theta} $$

分离变量得：

$$ \frac{dr}{r\tan\theta} = -d\alpha $$

将<2>代入得：

$$ \frac{L \, dr}{r\sqrt{2mE\left[\left(r+\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 + \frac{L^2}{2mE}\right]}} = -d\alpha \quad \text{<3>} $$

令 $u = \frac{1}{r}$，得：

$$ \frac{L\left(-\frac{du}{u^2}\right)}{\frac{1}{u}\sqrt{2mE\left[\left(\frac{1}{u}+\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2 + \frac{L^2}{2mE}\right]}} = -d\alpha $$

化简得：

$$ \frac{du}{\sqrt{\frac{2mE}{L^2} + \left(\frac{GmM}{L^2}\right)^2 - \left(u - \frac{GmM}{L^2}\right)^2}} = d\alpha \quad \text{<4>} $$

令 $u - \frac{GmM}{L^2} = \sqrt{\frac{2mE}{L^2} + \left(\frac{GmM}{L^2}\right)^2} \cos\beta$，得 $d\beta = -d\alpha$。

$$ \therefore u - \frac{GmM}{L^2} = \sqrt{\frac{2mE}{L^2} + \left(\frac{GmM}{L^2}\right)^2} \cos\alpha $$

将 $u = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}$ 和 $\cos\alpha = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}$ 代入上式，得：

$$ \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} = \frac{GmM}{L^2} + \frac{\sqrt{2mEL^2 + (GmM)^2}}{L^2} \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \quad \text{<5>} $$

整理得：

$$ \frac{y^2}{-\frac{L^2}{2mE}} + \frac{\left(x - \frac{\sqrt{(GmM)^2+2mEL^2}}{2mE}\right)^2}{\left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2} = 1 \quad \text{<6>} $$

1. 若 $E = 0$：

对于<5>式有：

$$ \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} = \frac{GmM}{L^2} + \frac{GmM}{L^2}\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} $$

化简得：

$$ 1 - \frac{2GmM}{L^2}x = \left(\frac{GmM}{L^2}\right)^2 y^2 $$

此为抛物线方程。<7>

2. 若 $E > 0$：

对于<6>式有 $-\frac{L^2}{2mE} < 0$，

$$ \therefore \frac{\left(x-\frac{\sqrt{(GmM)^2+2mEL^2}}{2mE}\right)^2}{\left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2} - \frac{y^2}{\left(\sqrt{\frac{L^2}{2mE}}\right)^2} = 1 $$

此为双曲线。<8>

3. 若 $E < 0$：

对于<6>式有 $-\frac{L^2}{2mE} > 0$，

$$ \therefore \frac{\left(x-\frac{\sqrt{(GmM)^2+2mEL^2}}{2mE}\right)^2}{\left(\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2} + \frac{y^2}{\left(\sqrt{-\frac{L^2}{2mE}}\right)^2} = 1 $$

此为椭圆。<9>

$\Delta$ 当 $(GmM)^2 + 2mEL^2 = 0$，即 $E = -\frac{(GmM)^2}{2mL^2} = -\frac{G^2mM^2}{2L^2}$ 时，

<9>式变为 $x^2 + y^2 = \left(-\frac{GmM}{2E}\right)^2$，此为圆方程。

$\Delta$ 当 $E < -\frac{GmM^2G}{2L^2}$ 时，此情况不存在（$\Delta < 0$）。
