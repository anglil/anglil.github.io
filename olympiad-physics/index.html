Olympiad Physics

A Rigorous Treatment of Competition Physics

Angli Liu

This textbook presents a complete, self-contained treatment of physics at the olympiad level. The exposition follows the classical tradition: precise definitions, rigorous derivations, worked examples, and challenging exercises. Every formula is derived from first principles; every diagram is drawn to convey physical intuition.

The material is divided into two parts. Part I covers mechanics — from kinematics through rotational dynamics, fluid mechanics, and wave phenomena. Part II covers thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, and modern physics including special relativity.
